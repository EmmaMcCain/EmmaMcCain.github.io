Butter Chicken Sauce

To make it into a meal, cook up bite-sized pieces of chicken and simmer them in the sauce until tender. Serve over fluffy basmati rice or scoop it up with warm naan. You can also toss in roasted veggies like cauliflower, chickpeas, or paneer for a plant-based twist.

Indgredients

Butter Chicken

  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 2 teaspoons ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh garlic, minced or paste
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons cayenne powder, to taste for spiciness
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon turmeic
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup havy cream
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, or sweetness to taste

Thai Red Cury

  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup red curry paste
  • 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 13-ounce can coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • salt, to taste
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water

Spicy "Vodka" Sauce

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh garlic, finely minced
  • 2/3 cup tomato paste
  • 1 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan, more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Alfredo Sauce

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon fresh garlic, minced
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 to 1 teaspoon salt, more to taste
  • 3/4 to 1 teaspoon black pepper, to taste
  • dash of nutmeg
  • 2 1/4 cups parmesan, freshly grated
  • 3 tablespoons cream cheese

Green Enchilada

  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 6 to 8 tomatillos, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1/2 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1 serrano chili, seeds and stem removed
  • 2 jalapeños, seeds and stem removed
  • 1 Anaheim pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, more to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 1/2 Chicken Broth
